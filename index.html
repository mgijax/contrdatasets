Mouse Genome Informatics: Other Contributed Data Sets and Tools

These data sets have been contributed to MGI by the authors. Currently they are not directly incorporated into the relational database. They are provided here as text or Excel files for the scientific community to view or download. We encourage contributions of such specialized important data sets that may be outside the structured information maintained in MGI as a vehicle for community collaborations. For data submission guidelines, see http://www.informatics.jax.org/mgihome/submissions/submissions_menu.shtml or send email to User Support.

NOTE: "Viewable Text" formatted pages are best viewed with your browser set to use a fixed-width font such as Courier.

  • FileMaker Templates for Marker Assisted Congenic Screening (MACS) Database
    MACS stores three classes of information (husbandry, genotype analysis and phenotype screening) that are central to the development of speed congenic and consomic lines. Downloadable files include a Help document of explanation and a series of FileMaker 5.0 templates. The FileMaker files have been tested on MAC OS (7.6.1 to 9.2) and Windows (95, 98, NT 4.0).

    Reference: J:82041, Collins, SC; Wallis, RH; Wallace K; McAteer M; Bihoreau MT; Gauguier D, Marker Assisted Congenic Screening (MACS): A Database Tool for the Efficient Production and Characterization of Congenic Lines., Mamm Genome 2003 (in press)
